What is kalyan matka?

Kalyan Matka began in the 1950s when people would bet on cotton prices, specifically the opening and closing rates. This practice, which started at the New York Cotton Exchange, eventually made its way to the Bombay Cotton Exchange. Although the New York Cotton Exchange discontinued this in 1961, people were already hooked and continued the practice. Gamblers found a way to restart it by using a piece of paper, and Kalyan Matka continued to grow into a thriving market where people invest and earn significant amounts.
